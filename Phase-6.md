Module: Financial & Billing Management

Context:
- School finance tracking

Responsibilities:
- Tuition invoices
- Extracurricular activity invoices
- Activity cost tracking
- Staff salary slips
- Maintenance and repair expenses

Rules:
- All invoices must have date, amount, description
- Link invoices to Student or Staff where applicable
- Financial calculations handled in BLO
- Prepare data for reporting

Use decimal for monetary values.
