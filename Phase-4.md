Module: Student Health & Vaccination Management

Context:
- Mandatory health tracking in kindergarten
- Government-required vaccination list

Responsibilities:
- Vaccine catalog (name, required age, description)
- Vaccination records:
  - status (done / not done)
  - vaccination date
  - medical unit
- Monthly student health records
- Teacher comments and evaluations

Rules:
- Vaccination records linked to Student
- Health records are monthly (Student + Month + Year)
- Validate no duplicate monthly health record
- Business rules in BLO

Generate extensible entities and services.
