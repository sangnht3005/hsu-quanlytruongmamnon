Module: Food, Ingredient & Menu Management

Context:
- Nutrition management for kindergarten
- Meals depend on attendance

Responsibilities:
- Supplier management
- Ingredient management (calories, unit price)
- Dish management:
  - ingredients list
  - total calories
  - total cost
  - selling price
- Daily menu management
- Meal ticket generation

Rules:
- Calculate calories and cost from ingredients
- Meal tickets generated only for present students
- Absent students do NOT receive meal tickets
- Meal ticket logic in BLO

Ensure data consistency and correct calculations.
